Meet the S-TEAM!

S-TEAM is a local company based in the Philippines and its name came from the initials of the last names of their 5 Executives; Sobrevega, the company’s Chief Operating Officer, Tomagan, the company’s Head Project Manager, Estrella, the company’s Chief Digital Innovation Officer, Alconcel, the company’s Chief Executive Officer, and Mariano, the company’s Senior Software Engineer. S-TEAM has built its foundation on resolving the lack of product stocks in physical stores in the Philippines. They developed an e-commerce website for local brands to develop a one-stop-shop that extends its accessibility to customers and explore the different products that are unavailable in physical stores.
